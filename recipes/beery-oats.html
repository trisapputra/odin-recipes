Berry Oats Recipe

Pureed mixed strawberry and blueberry swirled into a steaming bowl of cooked oats
to make this swirl variation on a breakfast classic.

Ingredients

  • uncooked oats
  • 1 cup water
  • Fresh blueberry and strawberry
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon plain nonfat yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened coconut
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Method

  1. In medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Stir in oats. Cook uncovered over medium heat, 1 minute for Quick Oats, 5 minutes for Old Fashioned Oats, stirring occasionally.
  2. Place berries, chia seeds, cinnamon, and ginger into blender or food processor container. Process until fruits are pureed. If mixture seems too thick, add water 1 tablespoon at a time to reach desired consistency. Swirl into bowl of cooked oats. Serve topped with yogurt, coconut, and honey.
